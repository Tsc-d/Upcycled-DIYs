Our Purpose

Project Zero is all about giving old things a fresh new glow-up. We're a bunch of creative students who saw how much waste happens every day and thought, "Why not turn trash into treasure?"

Whether it's transforming a boring old tee into a cute ribbon top or making eco-friendly jewelry out of paper, we believe upcycling can be fun *and* kind to the planet.
